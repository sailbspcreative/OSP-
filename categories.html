Scrap Categories

We accept a wide variety of recyclable materials.

Cardboard & Paper
Old newspapers, magazines, cardboard boxes, and office paper.
Plastics
PET bottles, PVC pipes, plastic chairs, and other hard plastics.
Metals
Iron, copper, brass, aluminum, and steel utensils or pipes.
E-Waste
Old computers, laptops, mobile phones, and electronic appliances.
Glass
Glass bottles, jars, and broken window glasses (packaged safely).
Batteries & Auto
Car batteries, inverter batteries, and old vehicle scrap parts.
